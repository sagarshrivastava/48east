What is it all about

Inspired by Asian cuisine, and marked by a multiplicity of regional and ethnical influences, 48 East strives to bring in the best of eclectic Asian gourmet food right at the doorsteps of people.

We have put in the best of our 50 years of combined experience as a star chef and a seasoned professional in the food business in ensuring that each meal is of the highest possible quality.

48 East offers interesting combinations of carefully researched, crafted and curated recipes; this is our ode to all the food lovers who want to make their everyday meal special.

We hope you will love this journey of gastronomic discovery as much as we did.
